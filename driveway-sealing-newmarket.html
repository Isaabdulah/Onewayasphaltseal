Driveway Sealing FAQs

How often should a driveway be sealed?
Most asphalt driveways should be sealed every 2 to 3 years, depending on usage, weather exposure, and the current condition of the surface.
What does driveway sealing help with?
Driveway sealing helps protect asphalt from oxidation, UV rays, moisture, and everyday wear while giving the surface a cleaner finished look.
How long does driveway sealing usually take?
Most residential driveway sealing jobs can be completed in a few hours, although drying time depends on temperature, shade, and weather conditions.
Can cracked asphalt still be sealed?
Yes, but larger cracks should usually be repaired first so the surface is properly prepared before the sealcoat is applied.
