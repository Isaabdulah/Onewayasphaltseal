Driveway Sealing FAQs

How often should I seal my driveway?
Most asphalt driveways should be sealed every 2 to 3 years, depending on traffic, weather exposure, and surface condition.
What are the benefits of driveway sealing?
Driveway sealing helps protect asphalt from oxidation, UV rays, moisture, and everyday wear while improving the look of the surface.
How long does driveway sealing take?
Most residential jobs can be completed in a few hours, although drying times depend on weather and site conditions.
Can you seal a driveway with cracks?
Yes, but larger cracks should usually be repaired first so the driveway is properly prepared before the sealcoat is applied.
